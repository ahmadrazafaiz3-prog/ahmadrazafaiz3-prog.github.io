# My Database Systems Journey Begins

![Database Study](/assets/images/database-study.jpg)

This semester was an important step in my Computer Engineering journey. One of the most interesting courses I studied was Database Systems. Under the guidance of Dr. Bilal Ahmad, I learned how databases are designed, organized, and used to solve real-world problems.

Before the midterm examination, we focused on ER modeling, keys, relationships, and SQL queries. These concepts helped me understand how data is stored and connected inside a database.

![SQL Practice](/assets/images/sql-practice.jpg)

After the midterm, we studied normalization and relational algebra. These topics improved my understanding of database design and efficiency. Looking back, this course taught me not only technical concepts but also the importance of practice and consistency.
